Rules specific to `ru.po`.

## Distinguish "identity" vs "account" terminology

- **"Identity"** (the user's Internet Identity — their profile/unique ID on the II platform) → **учётная запись**
- **"Account"** (an app-specific login created within II, or a third-party account like Google/Apple) → **аккаунт**

Do not use идентификатор for "identity". Do not use учётная запись for "account".
